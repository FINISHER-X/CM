ABOUT CUSTOMMADE

Our Story.
Your Inspiration.

Custommade is a thoughtfully curated journal and stationery brand dedicated to helping people

capture life’s moments, organize their thoughts, and live with greater intention.

Our journals are thoughtfully designed to be more than notebooks; they are companions for

reflection, planning, creativity, prayer, goal-setting, and personal growth. Each collection is

created with warmth, simplicity, and purpose, inviting you to slow down, write freely, and

embrace the beauty of your journey.

OUR DESIRE
Our desire is to create products that become companions for your everyday moments, whether
you’re planning your week, journaling your thoughts, recording answered prayers, brainstorming
your next big idea, or gifting someone a meaningful keepsake.
